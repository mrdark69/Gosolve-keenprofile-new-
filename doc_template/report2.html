About Your Current Job Fit Score

The current job fit score represents how much your current job role fit with you. The score vary between 0 to 100, if the score high (score between 71 to 100), it means the job that you’re working today is very fit with your Working Geniuses, even if you need to change company, you can still move to work in the same job function that similar to the current one. However, if the score is low (score between 0 to 50), it is the sign that you should find a new job opportunity as soon as possible, either from the current company or the new company.

After the information about the current job fit score, you can also see the details of your Working Geniuses compare with your current job role. The information will represent you on which Working Geniuses are used frequently at your work, and which one is rarely required. If your Top 7 are required in your job role, then it’s good for you, but if you rarely use them, then you will bore and cannot perform well. In contrast to the Bottom 4, if your job requires them a lot, it means that you are having a career problem that need actions soon. It’s ok for you to focus on just Top 7 and Bottom 4, and ignore the Genius items in between.

About Your
Current Company Fit Score

The current job fit score The current company fit score represents how much your current company fit with you. The score is the result of the matching between your Working Philosophies and the company’s culture. The score vary between 0 to 100, if the score is high (score between 61 to 100), it means that there is a high chance for you to get support from everyone and be happy to work here. But if the score is low (score between 0 to 40), even if your current Job Function fits with you (by looking at The Current Job Fit Score), you should find a way to pursue the new opportunity with the new company.

After the information of the current company fit score, you can also see the comparison details between your Working Philosophies and your company’s culture - item by item. Which item that your Working Philosophy and company’s culture different more than one level, it means that item does not fit between you and company.

Know more about “Working Traits”

What are types of work that fit with you?

After you know about your Working Geniuses, the next question is How should you use your Geniuses? What types of work that fit with you? After you know your Working Geniuses and Working Traits, then you can identify the job-functions that fit with you (You can get this information from the report - The Right Job-Functions Report). There are four themes of Working Traits:

Working Traits

If your Trait is Inventor Innovation, New Product/Service, New Business Model, New Startup Business; these things are not just your strategy, but it's in your blood. You like to contribute your Geniuses, time and energy to create new things. You never feel reluctant to build something from nothing. If you have to create something new every day, you will never feel it like work, but you will feel more like your hobby.
If your Trait is Promoter For you, it's always has a way to sell and steal market share from your competitors. You know that it's not just about the product that makes you win, but it's also about the effectiveness of sales and marketing, and this is the area that you could win over your competitors.
If your Trait is Optimizer You are always smarter than others in working efficiency and effectively. You always see gaps and possibilities to improve system and operation. For you, nothing is perfect; everything could be improved. You are willing to spend your time and energy to change people's behavior to do what you want. If you are working in the right role, you will turn the ordinary thing into the special thing.
If your Trait is Controller You're always a person who ensures that everyone is going to the right direction. Accuracy, Quality and on Schedule is your nature. You are willing to use your Geniuses to execute and carefully review day-to-day progress until your team can achieve the plan. You know the truth that strategy is nothing if you can't execute it well. The main factor that could define the winner is the effectiveness of the execution.

Know more about
“Working Philosophies”

What culture & environment that fit with you?

The last puzzle that you need to know to design strategy to your career path is What businesses and industries fit with you? What kind of culture and environment that you can perform well with the rich of supports? Although there are plenty of industries in global, we can categorize business industries into the three main groups:

Working Philosophies

If your Philosophy isInnovation You prefer to work in the environment that allows you to trial and error, the environment that not point the finger to the failed projects, but motivate everyone to fail fast to learn as much as possible from the failure. You ok to deal with uncertainty and unpredictable projects, because you know that your projects have never been implemented from anywhere before. You're happy to work with people who have high responsive to changes. One of the key performance metrics in your environment is Speed because of everyone aware that the first one in the market likely to be the winner for innovation market.
If your Philosophy is Service Excellence You fit with the environment which is customer-centric. People around you believe in creating the wow experience to customers. Customer Engagement and long-term customer relationship are the keys to success. Everything needs to be planned and organized well to provide beyond expectation service to customers. Everyone's also ok if they need to allow some flexibility in the process to satisfy customers. Perfect is an expectation to your environment. Everyone will do everything to increase customer retention rate and boost the number of repeat customers.
If your Philosophy isCost & Efficiency You belong to the environment that always can predictable and have solid planning. People around you believe in Quality and Efficiency. Cost is always the primary factor in any significant decision. Taking many risks is not make sense for this environment. Here, star employee is not a person who is good at taking risks, but a person who superb in risk management. Cost reduction is the main focus of everyone because costing is the key factor that can predict future for this business.
